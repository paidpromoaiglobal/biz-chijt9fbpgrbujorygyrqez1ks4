About Us

SURIYA DENTAL DEPOT AND EQUIPMENTS

Suriya Dental Depot and Equipments is a trusted name in dental supplies and equipment based in Sholinganallur, Chennai. Serving dental professionals across the region, we offer a comprehensive range of dental materials, chairs, instruments, and accessories at competitive prices. Our store is conveniently located at Kumaran Nagar, TNHB, making us easily accessible to clinics and practitioners across South Chennai and Pondicherry Pattai.

We are committed to providing genuine products and maintaining a reliable supply of spares and consumables that dental professionals depend on every day. Whether you are setting up a new clinic or maintaining an existing one, Suriya Dental Depot is your go-to destination for authentic dental equipment and prompt after-sales support. We are open six days a week with extended hours to serve the needs of busy dental practitioners.
